About RE/MAX

Our Story

RE/MAX is a global real estate network of more than 8000 franchisee-owned and operated offices with over 125,000 Sales Associates. India is the 73rd nation in the global network of RE/MAX International. In the year 2009, Samir Chopra initiated RE/MAX as a revolution in the real estate industry of India. Since then, RE/MAX India is rapidly expanding its growth opportunities and capturing the future to become an undisputed market leader in real estate transactions.
